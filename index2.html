About INCLEN

Global Health Research Organization

The INCLEN Trust International is a ‘not for profit’ research organization conducting collaborative, multi-disciplinary studies on high priority global health issues. Our core functional units consist of Clinical Epidemiology Units (CEU) and Clinical Epidemiology Research Training Centre (CERTC’s) located in 89 academic institutions in 34 countries.

Presently, the INCLEN Executive Office (IEO) is located in New Delhi and collaborates with over 218 institutes in India, maintaining strong strategic relationships with government departments like MOHFW, ICMR, and professional bodies. We are recognized as a Scientific and Industrial Research Organization (SIRO) with FCRA approvals.

In 2009, IEO established SOMAARTH, a Demographic, Development and Environmental Surveillance Site (DDESS) in Palwal district, Haryana. It covers 51 contiguous rural villages encompassing over 200,000 population, operating under an MOU with the Government of Haryana.
